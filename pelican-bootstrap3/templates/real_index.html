OUR PREACHING

We believe that the preaching of God’s word is his chosen means to convert sinners and build up his people. We believe in verse-by-verse exposition and application of the Bible, otherwise known as expository preaching.


OUR WORSHIP

We seek to worship according to God’s word. Our worship service is what most people would call “traditional.” We don’t believe that makes it any less joyful or vibrant. We love worshipping God together. We sing hymns and psalms, we pray, and we listen to God’s word. In other words, we try to do what Christians have done for 2000 years.

OUR Pastor

Matthew Holst, a native of Wales, was installed as senior pastor of Shiloh in June 2016. He is a 2008 graduate of Greenville Presbyterian Theological Seminary. His wife, Stacy is a native of Greenville SC and together they have four children.

OUR Children

We believe that children are a blessing from the Lord! We provide a clean, welcoming nursery for any children age two and under.

We also encourage children to attend the worship service with their parents.

Every Sunday evening at 4:45 a Shiloh elder and his wife teach Candychism. Children memorize the Children’s Catechism and receive candy at the end!

Our Denomination

Orthodox Presbyterian Church

We're the Orthodox Presbyterian Church. Our purpose is simple:

  • to bring glory to God through our churches and individual lives
  • to make known to the world the good news of salvation through Jesus Christ
